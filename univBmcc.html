Borough of Manhattan Community College

Motto: "Start Here, Go Anywhere"

Purpose:
The motto emphasizes the college's role as a starting point for students' educational and career journeys, regardless of their aspirations.

Mission:
BMCC strives to increase degree completion, successful transfer, career achievement, and service and leadership within its community, New York City, and beyond.

Values:
BMCC is committed to diversity, inclusivity, and mutual respect for all members of the community.

Location:
BMCC is located in downtown Manhattan, close to artistic communities like Tribeca and SoHo, the financial district, and City Hall.

Academic Programs:
BMCC offers associate degrees in various fields, including STEM, business, criminal justice, and nursing, with flexible scheduling options.

Student Body:
BMCC welcomes a diverse student body, including recent high school graduates and seasoned professionals, from 105 different countries with more than 100 languages spoken.

Support Services: BMCC provides robust services to support students' success, including academic advisement, financial aid, child care, tutoring, and counseling.
